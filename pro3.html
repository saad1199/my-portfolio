Examination System (FDE)

Automated Manual System of taking Examinations in Federal Government Institutes. 430 Institutes with 45000 Students enrolled in this system. From Registration of students to Dispatching Result Cards, Including Fee collection module as well as Marking Module deployed under the umbrella of MoFEPT.

Client: Government of Pakistan (FDE)
Type: Enterprise Web Application
Year: 2022-2023
Preview: exams.fde.gov.pk
